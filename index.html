About Tower Strike

Tower Strike is a strategic Roblox tower defense game that brings intense battles and tactical challenges. Defend your base from waves of enemies and enjoy unique gameplay mechanics. Join our Discord community and be a part of the action. The game will be released soon, so stay tuned!

Game Features

  • Multiple tower types with unique abilities

  • Multiplayer mode for cooperative play
  • Regular updates with new content
